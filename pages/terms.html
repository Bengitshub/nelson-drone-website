Terms of service

How a job with us works on paper — written in plain English so both sides know where they stand before anything leaves the ground.

Working draft · 23 July 2026 · to be reviewed before launch

The short version

You request a time, we check the site, and you get a written quote. Once you're happy, the booking is confirmed. We fly, you get a download link, and a Stripe invoice follows. Weather moves cost nothing and nobody pays before delivery. The rest of this page is just the detail behind those sentences.

Quotes and booking requests

Choosing a time on the booking page sends us a request — it doesn't create a booking. A booking exists once we've checked the site and confirmed it with you.

  • The written quote fixes the price. It states the total payable, and that's the number on the invoice unless we agree a change in writing.
  • We need at least 48 hours' notice on any requested slot.

Weather and rescheduling

If conditions on the day aren't safe and useful to fly in, we move the shoot to the next workable window at no charge. A weather move is never treated as a cancellation by you, and either side can rebook without penalty.

Site access and permissions

You arrange permission from the landowner or occupier of the shoot location, and access on the day. We handle the airspace side and the legality of the flight itself.

If permission turns out to be missing when we arrive, we reschedule — and travel for the wasted trip may be charged.

Flight decisions

The call on safety and legality is the pilot's, and it's final. If a location can't be flown legally or safely, we say so. We then either re-plan that part of the job with you or cancel it — you're not charged for anything that wasn't flown.

Deliverables and revisions

You get what's listed in the package you booked. The short highlight includes one revision; the other packages are delivered as edited.

Ask for changes within 7 days of delivery — that window is a placeholder and may be adjusted before launch. Extra revisions and extra formats are quoted as add-ons.

Payment

After delivery we send a Stripe invoice, payable within 7 days (placeholder term). Pay by card or bank transfer through the invoice link. No payment is taken on this website, before the shoot or on site.

If an invoice sits overdue, we may pause new bookings until it's settled.

Usage rights

Once the invoice is paid, you get a broad licence to use the delivered media for your business, marketing and listings. We keep the copyright.

We may show the work in our own portfolio. If you'd rather we didn't, tell us in writing and we won't.

Liability

Nothing in these terms limits your rights under the Consumer Guarantees Act where it applies. Beyond that, our liability for a job is capped at the amount you paid for it. This wording is a placeholder and is flagged for legal review before launch.
