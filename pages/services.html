Straight up

What I don't do

I'm one pilot with a camera drone, not a survey firm or a film crew. Saying no to the jobs below keeps my quotes honest and my delivery dates real — and points you to the right person faster.

  • Survey-grade mapping or certified measurement
  • Thermal or engineering inspection reports
  • Full wedding films
  • Scripted commercial productions
  • Interviews, voiceover or audio recording
  • Motion graphics and visual effects
  • Film-grade colour grading
  • Any promise your video will go viral
