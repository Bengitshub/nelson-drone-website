The four services

What each one actually delivers

Aerial photography

Edited high-resolution stills of homes, land, buildings and coastline, delivered as hi-res files plus web-sized copies ready to upload.

Choose it when a picture does the job on its own — a property listing, land for sale, or a record of a site shot from angles no ladder reaches.

  • 10–15 edited stills per shoot
  • Hi-res files plus web-sized copies
  • Property, land, buildings and coastline
  • Shot for the use you name — listing, website or record
Sample · residential aerial stills

Drone video

Video comes in two forms. Trimmed clips are colour-corrected and cut to length, with no music or titles — made for your own editor or social team to build on.

The short highlight is the finished article: a 20–45 second edit with one music track, clean pacing and a simple title or logo card. One format, one revision. Pick clips if someone edits for you; pick the highlight if you want a file you can post as-is.

  • Trimmed, colour-corrected clips — no music, no titles
  • Or a 20–45 s edited highlight with one music track
  • Simple title or logo card on the edited version
  • One format, one revision included
Sample · trimmed clip still

FPV & 360 capture

FPV flying takes the camera through, around and over things a standard drone hovers back from — one continuous take with close, fast movement. The 360 camera records in every direction at once, so shots can be reframed after the flight.

It's an add-on capability, not a gimmick. Choose it when a single moving shot explains a space better than a set of stills — a lodge walkthrough, a trail, a working site.

  • Continuous fly-throughs in one take
  • Close movement past, under and through structures
  • 360 footage that can be reframed after the flight
  • Quoted as part of a photo or video package
Sample · FPV fly-through frame

Progress shoots

We photograph the same viewpoints on a repeat schedule, so every visit lines up with the last. Files arrive in date-labelled folders — easy to compare, easy to hand to a client, a lender or a council.

Choose it for builds, subdivisions and long-running projects. We recommend three or more visits — that's when the change starts to show properly.

  • Same viewpoints, revisited on schedule
  • Stills and progress clips from each visit
  • Date-labelled folders for side-by-side comparison
  • 3+ visits recommended
