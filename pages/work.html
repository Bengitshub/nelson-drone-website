The plan for this page

We'd rather run an honest placeholder than pass off borrowed footage as our own. Here's exactly how these tiles become real work.

Before launch we're flying four controlled sample shoots around Nelson and Tasman — each one planned, checked and delivered exactly like a paid job:

  • A home or lifestyle block — the classic property brief, stills first.
  • An accommodation spot or venue — buildings, grounds and the setting around them.
  • A construction or commercial site — the progress-shoot treatment, date-labelled.
  • Something moving — a boat, a vehicle or outdoor sport, flown FPV.

Each shoot delivers edited stills, clean trimmed clips and a short highlight — the same files a client would get, organised the same way. The site goes live on that real footage. No stock, no licensed filler.

Want to be one of the first subjects?

If you've got a property, venue, build or boat that would suit one of those four shoots, get in touch — we're a small local operation and early subjects help us both. You get the finished files; we get real work to show here, with your permission.
