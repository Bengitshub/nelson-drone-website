Privacy policy

What information we collect when you request a shoot, what our drone captures from the air, and what you can ask us to do about both.

Working draft · 23 July 2026 · to be reviewed before launch

Who we are

Nelson Drone Co. is a one-pilot aerial photography and video service based in Nelson, New Zealand, working across Nelson, Tasman and Marlborough. This policy covers the personal information we collect when you use this website or hire us for a shoot.

The operating entity will be confirmed before launch. When it is, this section will name the legal entity responsible for your information.

What we collect

We keep it small and practical. The information we hold is:

  • Booking requests — your name, email, phone number, the site address and the job details, entered through Cal.com when you request a shoot.
  • Correspondence — emails and phone notes about your job, kept so the quote and the scope stay accurate.
  • Payment records — invoices and receipts handled by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Site analytics — basic anonymous visit counts, if analytics is enabled. Nothing that identifies you personally.

Aerial footage and other people

A drone camera doesn't stop at the boundary fence. A shoot can incidentally capture neighbouring properties, vehicles, or people who happen to be nearby.

We plan every flight to keep that to a minimum — flight paths, camera angles and timing are chosen to cover the client's site, not the places next to it. Where footage does include other people or their property, we handle it as personal information under the NZ Privacy Act 2020.

If you believe you or your property appear in our footage, email us. We'll review the material and, where it's reasonable, blur or delete it.

How we use your information

We use what we collect to quote your job, schedule the flight, deliver your files, invoice you after delivery, and keep the legal and safety records a drone operation has to keep.

We don't sell or rent personal information, and we don't run marketing lists at launch. If that ever changes, this policy changes first.

Storage and retention

Project files and finished deliverables are kept in cloud storage, and you receive them as a download link.

We keep project media for at least 12 months so clients can re-download their files if a link expires or a drive fails. Exact retention terms will be finalised before launch.

Services we rely on

A small set of third-party services helps us run the business. Each one processes only what it needs to do its job:

  • Cal.com — takes booking requests and the details you enter with them.
  • Stripe — issues invoices and processes card and bank-transfer payments.
  • Google Calendar — holds the schedule for confirmed shoots.
  • Cloud file delivery — stores project files and serves your download links.
  • Netlify — hosts this website.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you, and ask us to correct it if it's wrong. Email us and we'll respond promptly.

If you're not happy with how we've handled something, raise it with us first — most problems are fixed quickly. If we can't sort it out, you can complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or about footage you think you appear in, go to hello@nelsondrone.co.nz. You'll be dealing with the pilot — the same person who handles the footage.
