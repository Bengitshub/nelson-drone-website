About

Local knowledge. Professional flight planning.

Nelson Drone Co. provides dedicated aerial photography and video across Nelson, Tasman and Marlborough. Every shoot is planned around the location, intended use and operating conditions — not simply whether the drone can take off.

The current capture platform is the DJI Avata 360, selected for flexible 360 imaging, smooth movement and FPV-style flight. Where a project needs a different camera platform or operating setup, that will be identified before quoting.

Good to know

Questions people ask

Can I book a shoot instantly?
You can request an available time online. The shoot is not confirmed until the location, airspace, weather and scope have been checked.
Do you provide edited video?
Yes. Choose selected ready-to-use clips or a simple 20–45 second highlight. Full commercial film production is not part of the standard launch service.
Can you provide photos as well as video?
Yes. Aerial photography is a core service and can be booked alone or with video.
What happens if the weather is unsuitable?
The shoot is moved to the next suitable agreed window. Final decisions are based on safe operating conditions, not only rain.
Where do you work?
Nelson and Richmond are the core local area. Tasman, Motueka, Golden Bay, Marlborough and wider South Island work can be quoted.
Can you fly anywhere?
No. Every location is subject to airspace, landowner, people, privacy, wildlife and operating-rule requirements.
