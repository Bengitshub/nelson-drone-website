The difference

One take. No cuts. Straight through.

Most aerial video is shot from a hovering camera drone — beautiful, but always at arm's length. FPV is flown like it's on rails: diving, chasing and threading through spaces in a single unbroken move.

That's the shot that stops the scroll — the one your audience feels. It's what turns a venue tour, a listing or an adventure promo into something people actually watch to the end.

  • Seamless exterior-to-interior fly-throughs
  • Dynamic reveals, dive shots & proximity flying
  • Fast chase footage — bikes, boats, kayaks, runners
  • Ducted, guarded props — safe to fly close to people & indoors
