About

Flown by a local who does the homework.

Nelson Drone Co. is a one-pilot operation based in Nelson. Before anything leaves the ground we check the airspace, the forecast, the access and who else will be around — because the difference between a good aerial shot and a wasted trip is planning, not luck.

We currently fly a DJI Avata 360: small, agile, and able to capture 360 footage that can be reframed into different shots after the flight. If a job needs a different platform, we say so before quoting rather than after.

Good to know

Before you ask

Is picking a time the same as booking?
Not quite. Choosing a slot sends us a request. We check the site, airspace and forecast, agree the scope with you, and then lock it in.
What do I actually get?
Depends on the package — edited stills, trimmed ready-to-use clips, or a short edited highlight with music. Everything arrives as a download link.
What if the weather turns?
We move the shoot to the next workable window at no charge. The call is about safe, useful flying conditions — not just rain.
How far will you travel?
Nelson and Richmond are home base. Motueka, Golden Bay, Marlborough and further afield are all quotable — travel is itemised in the quote.
Are there places you can't fly?
Yes. Airspace, landowner permission, people, privacy and wildlife rules all apply. That's exactly what we check before confirming.
How do I pay?
After delivery we send a Stripe invoice — pay online by card or bank transfer. No payment is taken on this website.
