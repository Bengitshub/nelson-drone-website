Send the request

Pick a slot and tell me where the job is, what you need captured, and how you'll use it.

2

I scout it from the desk

Airspace, access, neighbours and the forecast all get a look. If something rules the site out, you'll hear it quickly.

Written quote

One fixed price for the flight, the editing and delivery. Accept it and the date is confirmed in the calendar.

4

Fly, deliver, invoice

You get a download link — photos in three working days, video in five — then a Stripe invoice to pay online by card or bank transfer. Nothing is charged on this site.

To speed things up

Have these ready

None of it is compulsory, but the more you can tell me up front, the faster I can come back with a real quote instead of more questions.

  • The site address, or a dropped pin
  • What you need captured — the house, the whole block, the build, an event
  • Where the media will be used — a listing, a website, social, a report
  • How flexible the date is — fixed, flexible by a few days, or flexible by weather
  • Whether you own or manage the site, or have the owner's permission
  • Anything unusual about access, people or animals on site
