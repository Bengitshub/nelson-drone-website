Before you request

What to have ready

The request form asks a handful of questions. Rough answers are fine — they just let us start the checks straight away.

  • The site address — or a pin and a description if it's rural land without one.
  • What needs capturing: stills, clips, a short highlight, or repeat progress visits.
  • Where the media will be used — listing, website, social, or project records.
  • How flexible your dates are, including whether the shoot can move with the weather.
  • Whether you own or manage the site, or have the owner's permission for us to fly there.
  • Anything unusual about access, or about people who'll be on site during the shoot.
