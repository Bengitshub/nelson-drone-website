Terms of service

The deal between us, written the way I'd want it explained to me.

Working draft · 23 July 2026 · for review before launch

1. The short version

You request a date. I check the site and the weather, then send you a written quote. You accept it, I fly, and I deliver your files by a download link. A Stripe invoice follows. If the weather won't play, we move the date for free. That's the whole shape of it — the rest is detail.

2. Requests and quotes

Checking a date on the booking page is a request, not a confirmed booking. A booking only exists once I've reviewed the job and confirmed it back to you. The written quote fixes the price and states the total payable. I ask for at least 48 hours' notice so there's time to do the checks properly.

3. The Weather Promise

If the window won't produce material worth paying for, I'll call it early — usually the day before — and we move to the next good window at no charge. A weather move is never treated as a cancellation, and rescheduling works both ways without penalty. You're paying for good pictures, not for a drone in the air on a bad day.

4. Access and permissions

You arrange permission from the landowner or occupier and make sure I can get to the site. I take care of airspace and the legality of the flight itself. If it turns out on the day that permission is missing, we'll reschedule, and travel may be charged for the wasted trip.

5. The pilot's call

My decision on what's safe and legal to fly is final. If part of a job can't be flown, I'll tell you plainly — and you won't pay for anything that didn't get flown.

6. Deliverables and revisions

You get what your package lists. The Short Cut includes one revision; other packages are capture and editing as described. Change requests are welcome within 7 days of delivery (a placeholder window, to be finalised). Extra revisions and extra formats are quoted as add-ons. Delivery targets — photos within three working days, video within five — are targets I aim for, stated honestly rather than guaranteed to the hour.

7. Payment

I send a Stripe invoice after your files are delivered, payable within 7 days (placeholder terms). Pay online by card or bank transfer through the invoice link. Nothing is charged through this website. Overdue invoices may pause any new bookings until they're settled.

8. Usage rights

Once the invoice is paid, you get a broad licence to use the delivered media for your own business, marketing and listings. I keep the copyright, and I may show the work in my own portfolio — unless you ask me in writing not to, in which case I won't.

9. Liability

Nothing in these terms limits any rights you have under the Consumer Guarantees Act where it applies. Otherwise, my liability for a job is capped at the amount you paid for it. This wording is a placeholder and will be reviewed by a professional before launch.

10. About this website

Details marked as placeholders — contact numbers, the legal entity, credentials, and the sample media — are still being finalised. Content on this site may change before launch.
