The Weather Promise

If the light's wrong, I'll call it early.

Drone work depends on suitable weather and visibility. I keep an eye on the forecast before every shoot and reschedule early if conditions are unsafe or unlikely to produce good results. Weather-related rescheduling is free — and the pilot's safety and legal call is always final.

The operator

Straight to the operator.

Haven Aerials is an independent Nelson drone operator — one experienced operator and one point of contact from planning through to delivery. You deal directly with the person flying your job, with extra support brought in where a flight calls for it.

The aircraft is a DJI Avata 360: high-resolution aerial stills, 8K 360° video and agile flight in one. I fly to the New Zealand drone rules, and if a site can't be flown safely or legally, I'll say so before quoting.

Good to know

Fair questions

Is requesting a shoot a commitment?
No — it sends me a request and holds the slot. The booking is only confirmed after I've checked the site and you've accepted the written quote.
What arrives at the end?
A download link, organised into folders: full-resolution stills, web-sized copies, and any clips or 360° outputs from your package. Photos land within three working days, video within five.
What happens when the weather turns bad?
I keep an eye on the forecast and reschedule early if the conditions won't produce good results. Weather-related rescheduling is free.
Can you fly my location?
Often, but I check first. Airspace near Nelson Airport, access, people and neighbouring properties all shape the plan — that's the feasibility check I do before quoting.
How does payment work?
Once you accept the written quote, I confirm the booking and send the invoice details by email. Payment terms are set out on each quote — no payment is taken through this website.
What's the 360° capture about?
The Avata 360 shoots high-resolution 360° stills and 8K 360° video, so I can create an interactive aerial viewpoint people can look around — handy for accommodation, tourism and lifestyle property. It's quoted per project.

Got a spot in mind?

Request a shoot and tell me what you're after — I'll check the flight and send a written quote before you commit to anything.
