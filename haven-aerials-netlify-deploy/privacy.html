Privacy policy

Plain English on what I collect when you get in touch or book a flight, and what I do with it.

Working draft · 23 July 2026 · for review before launch

1. Who I am

Haven Aerials is a one-pilot drone photography and videography service based in Nelson, New Zealand. The operating legal entity will be confirmed before launch and named here. You can reach me any time at hello@havenaerials.nz.

2. What I collect

  • Booking requests made through the booking calendar (Cal.com): your name, email, phone, the site address and the details you give me about the job.
  • Correspondence — the emails and phone calls we exchange about your shoot.
  • Payment records handled through a third-party payment provider. I can see that an invoice was paid and the basic details on it; I never see or store your full card number.
  • Basic website analytics, anonymous and aggregate, if analytics is switched on.

3. Aerial footage and other people

This is the part that's specific to drone work, so I want to be clear about it. A flight can incidentally capture neighbouring properties or people who happen to be nearby. I plan flight paths to keep that to a minimum, and I handle all footage in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

If you believe you appear in footage I've captured and you'd like it reviewed, blurred or deleted, email me and I'll sort it out.

4. How I use your information

Only to do the job: preparing quotes, scheduling and confirming your shoot, delivering your files, invoicing, and keeping the safety and legal records I'm required to keep. I don't sell or rent personal information, and I don't run marketing lists.

5. Storage and retention

Project files and deliverables are held in cloud storage, and you get an organised download link. I keep project media for at least twelve months so you can re-download if you need to. Final retention terms will be confirmed before launch.

6. The services I rely on

A few trusted services help me run the business, and each only handles what it needs to:

  • Cal.com — the booking calendar.
  • A payment provider — invoicing and payment (the specific provider will be confirmed before launch).
  • Google Calendar — scheduling and conflict checks.
  • Cloud file delivery — sending you your finished files.
  • Netlify — hosting this website.

7. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information I hold about you and to correct it. Raise anything with me first at hello@havenaerials.nz. If you're not satisfied with how I've handled it, you can contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@havenaerials.nz or call 03 000 0000.
