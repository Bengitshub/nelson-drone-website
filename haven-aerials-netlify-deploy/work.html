Before I take paid work

Four real shoots, on the calendar now.

I don't want to launch on a stock library and a promise. So before I take a cent, I'm flying four controlled portfolio shoots across the kinds of jobs I'll actually be asked for:

  • A home or lifestyle block, shot for a real-estate listing
  • An accommodation or venue, the way a guest first sees it
  • A build site, mid-project, for a progress set
  • Something moving on the water — a boat or the coastline

Each one delivers the full spread: edited stills, clean clips, and a short edited piece. When they're done, they replace the samples above, and this page goes live on real footage.
