What we do

Drone Videography Services in Nelson & Tasman

Every film we make is built around one thing a normal camera drone can't do: fly properly. Our FPV drone doesn't hover at arm's length — it dives off a ridgeline, threads through a doorway and sweeps around a room in one unbroken take. That's the shot people actually watch to the end.

Below are the drone videography services we offer across Nelson, Richmond, Motueka, the wider Tasman and Marlborough. Pick the format that fits your business, or ask for something custom. Every job is quoted to your location and scope — no fixed packages, no surprises.

One continuous take — the FPV difference

Standard aerial video is filmed from a hovering camera drone: lovely, but always looking on from a distance. FPV is flown like it's on rails — diving, chasing and threading through tight spaces in a single continuous move. Guarded props mean we can fly close to people and indoors, so every service below can go places a normal drone can't.

  • Seamless exterior-to-interior fly-throughs, no cuts
  • Dive shots, dynamic reveals and proximity flying
  • Fast chase footage — bikes, boats, kayaks, runners
  • Safe to fly indoors and close to guests

Venue & hospitality fly-throughs

This is our flagship format. One immersive shot lifts off outside your restaurant, cellar door, gym or function space, flies through the entrance and moves through the room like a guest walking in — so people feel the atmosphere before they ever arrive. It's the drone videography Nelson hospitality operators use to turn a website and social feed into a reason to book.

  • Restaurants, cafés and bars
  • Wineries and cellar doors
  • Gyms, studios and event spaces

Tourism & adventure films

Mountain biking, kayaking, walking tracks, wineries and the Nelson coast — filmed with the speed and motion the activity deserves. FPV chases a rider down the trail and skims the water beside the kayak, giving tourism operators a hero reel that sells the experience, not just the view. Perfect for operators who need marketing that moves as fast as their guests do.

Real estate drone video

A standard aerial photo shows a roofline. An FPV listing film flies over the property, drops down and carries straight through the front door in one continuous move — outside to inside, without a cut. It's the premium option that lifts a listing above the pack and holds a buyer's attention right through.

Weddings & events

Cinematic reveals a ground camera simply can't get — over the ceremony, through the trees and across the dance floor in one sweeping shot. We fly with guarded props and a spotter so it's safe around your guests, and we capture the day without getting in the way of it. A free reschedule if the weather turns means your date is never at the mercy of the wind.

Business & social reels

Short, scroll-stopping video for your Instagram, TikTok and website, built to grab attention in the first second. Book a one-off reel or a regular content package that keeps fresh footage coming through the year. Ideal for tradespeople, retailers, gyms and any local business that lives on social.

Aerial photography — add-on

Need stills as well as video? We'll capture high-angle aerial photography to round out any shoot — hero images for your website, listings or marketing. This runs off a standard camera drone alongside the FPV film, so you get both in a single visit.

FAQ

Questions & answers

Which drone service is right for my business?

If you run a venue, a fly-through is usually the standout. Real estate agents lean on listing films, tourism operators on adventure reels, and most other businesses on short social content. Tell us what you're marketing and we'll recommend the format — or combine a few.

Do you offer set packages or fixed prices?

No fixed packages. Every job is quoted to your location, the shoot and the edit you need, so you only pay for what fits. Get in touch and we'll send a quick, clear price.

Can I combine services in one shoot?

Often, yes. If your site suits it, we can capture the FPV film and add aerial photography in the same visit, or shoot both a hero reel and short social cuts from one booking. We'll map it out when we quote.

Do you cover my area?

We work across Nelson, Richmond, Motueka, the wider Tasman district and Marlborough. Got a spot further out? Ask — we travel for the right project.
