FAQ

Questions & answers

How do I book a shoot?

Two ways: call us for the quickest answer, or send a quick enquiry with the location, the kind of job and rough dates. We'll confirm your date, sort the airspace, shoot, then email the invoice afterwards. There's no online payment to deal with.

How fast will you get back to me?

We aim to reply within a day, and often the same day if you phone. Dates book up around weekends and good weather, so it pays to get in touch early.

Do I have to pay online to book?

No. Booking a date costs nothing upfront. We complete the shoot and then email you a simple invoice through Stripe — nothing to pay on the website itself.

What areas do you cover?

Nelson, Richmond, Motueka, the wider Tasman district and Marlborough. If your location is further out, ask us — we travel for the right project.

What if the weather isn't good on the day?

Drones don't fly safely in strong wind or rain, so if conditions aren't right we simply reschedule at no charge. Nelson gives us plenty of good-weather days to work with.
