Social media video in Nelson that stops the scroll

Your customers are already on their phones — thumbs moving, feeds flying past. A great short clip is what makes them stop. That's the job of social media video, and it's what we shoot for Nelson and Tasman businesses who'd rather run their business than wrestle with a camera.

Whether you need one hero clip or a steady drip of content across Instagram, TikTok and Facebook, we handle the flying, the filming and the edit. You get footage that looks like your place — and moves like nothing else in the feed.

Why social media video works for local business

A still photo tells people what your place looks like. A short video shows them what it feels like to be there — and the feeds reward it. Instagram, TikTok and Facebook all push video harder than anything else, so a good short-form clip gets in front of more locals for the same effort. For a small business, that's about the best-value reach going.

  • Video gets more reach than photos on every major platform
  • One shoot fuels weeks of posts, not just a single one
  • Shows your atmosphere, not just your address
  • Built to land in the first two seconds, before the thumb moves on

The FPV edge in a 15-second reel

This is where we're different. A normal drone hovers and watches from a distance. Ours flies the shot — diving in off the street, through the door, around the room and back out in one unbroken move. On a small screen, that motion is magnetic. It's the kind of clip people watch twice and send to a mate.

FAQ

Questions & answers

How much does a social media video cost?

Every job is quoted to what you need — the location, how many clips and how often. Get in touch and we'll give you a straight price. We confirm the date, do the shoot, then email the invoice afterwards, so there's no online payment to deal with.

How many videos come in a monthly package?

As many as makes sense for your business — we agree the number and rhythm up front, so you know exactly what's landing each month. It's a simple arrangement built around your channels, with no surprises.

Do I need to be on camera?

Not at all. FPV is about motion and place — your space, your product, your team in action. If you want faces and talking to camera we can work that in, but plenty of great reels have neither.

Which platforms and formats do you deliver for?

Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook and Stories — mainly vertical 9:16, with square or landscape cut-downs on request. Everything arrives online, ready to upload straight to your feed.

Do you cover my area?

We're set up for Nelson and the Tasman region — Nelson, Richmond, Motueka and the wider Tasman, plus Marlborough. Somewhere further out? Ask us — we travel for the right project.
