A photo shows a house. An FPV film shows the whole property in one unbroken move — lifting off the street, sweeping the roofline, dropping through the front door and gliding room to room. It's the kind of listing that makes buyers stop scrolling and book a viewing.

That's real estate drone video done properly across Nelson and Tasman: cinematic, immersive, and built to sell. Standard aerial photos have their place, but a one-take fly-through gives a property something the rest of the portal simply doesn't have.

One take, from the sky to the sofa

Most aerial listing shots come from a drone hovering high overhead — handy for the section and boundaries, but always at arm's length. FPV is different. We fly one continuous shot that starts above the property, threads through the entrance and moves through the living spaces, so buyers get a real feel for flow, light and lifestyle before they ever step inside.

  • Exterior-to-interior in a single unbroken take
  • Reveals the site, aspect and outlook from the air
  • Shows how the rooms connect — the walk-through, elevated
  • Guarded props let us fly safely indoors and close to the home

Aerial photo stills, same shoot

Need stills for the portal, the brochure and the window card too? We can capture high-angle aerial photography on the same visit as an add-on — clean, well-composed shots of the home, the section and the surrounding streetscape. One booking, film and photos sorted together.

Built for Nelson, Richmond, Motueka and wider Tasman

We shoot listings right across the region — lifestyle blocks on the Waimea plains, townhouses in the city, coastal homes out Motueka and Marlborough way. Goggled FPV flies with a pilot and a spotter, kept safe and legal under CAA Part 101.

FAQ

Questions & answers

Can the drone really fly inside a home?

Yes — that's the FPV speciality. Guarded props let us move safely through the front door, down hallways and around rooms for a seamless interior fly-through a standard camera drone can't manage.

Do I get photos as well as video?

Aerial photo stills are available as an add-on on the same shoot, so you can have both the hero film and high-angle images of the property and section without booking twice.

Can you legally fly in Nelson near the airport?

Much of Nelson, Richmond and the Waimea plains sit under Nelson Airport's controlled airspace. We arrange the air-traffic authorisation through AirShare, or use low-level shielded flying where it applies, so the airspace side is handled before the shoot.

How much does a listing film cost?

Every job is quoted to the property, the location and what you need. Get in touch for a quick price — and to keep it simple, we email the invoice after the shoot rather than taking payment online.

What if the weather's bad on the day?

Drones don't fly in strong wind or rain, so if conditions aren't safe we reschedule at no charge. Nelson's climate gives us plenty of good-weather days to work with.
