Tourism drone video in Nelson, done properly

We're a small, local FPV operation — Nelson-based, flown by a professional pilot with a spotter on every shoot, and fully across the rules. Much of Nelson, Richmond and the Waimea plains sits under Nelson Airport's controlled airspace, so we sort the AirShare authorisation or use low-level shielded flying before we ever turn up. You get the footage; we carry the paperwork.

  • CAA Part 101 compliant — no pilot licence needed for us to film commercially
  • Pilot plus spotter on site, as goggled FPV requires
  • Controlled-airspace authorisations handled for you
  • Free reschedule if wind or rain makes it unsafe to fly

DOC permits and land access — we sort it before we fly

Here's the honest bit. Filming inside a national park like Abel Tasman needs a concession or permit from the Department of Conservation, and that takes real lead time — it's a plan-ahead job, not a next-week one. We'll tell you straight whether a location is doable, apply for the right DOC or private-land consent where it's granted, and plan around the spots that aren't quick. And there's no shortage of jaw-dropping coast, forest and farmland just outside the parks that flies beautifully with far less paperwork.

  • We arrange DOC concessions and private land access where they're needed
  • National-park work is possible but needs permits and lead time — book early
  • We'll suggest equally stunning locations that fly with fewer consents

FAQ

Questions & answers

Can you film adventure activities like mountain biking and kayaking?

Yes — that's exactly what FPV is built for. We fly chase footage that keeps pace with your riders, paddlers or runners, then edit it into a hero reel plus short cuts sized for social. Point us at the activity and we'll get in among it.

Can you film in Abel Tasman National Park?

National-park filming needs a concession or permit from DOC, which takes lead time, so it's a plan-ahead project rather than a next-week one. We'll be upfront about what's possible, arrange the right consent where it's granted, and can suggest stunning locations outside the park boundary that fly with far less paperwork.

How much does a tourism or winery reel cost?

Every job is quoted to your location, the story and how much footage you need. Get in touch and we'll send a quick price. Booking is simple: we confirm the date, do the shoot, then email your invoice afterwards — no online payment to wrestle with.

What areas do you cover?

Nelson, Richmond, Motueka, the wider Tasman district and Marlborough. Got a spot further out for the right project? Ask us.

What happens if the weather turns on shoot day?

Drones don't fly safely in strong wind or rain, so if conditions aren't right we simply reschedule at no charge. Nelson gives us plenty of good-weather days to work with.
